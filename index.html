Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, is home to the world's fastest and most reliable transportation system. Located in the heart of the city, it provides access to various modes of transportation, including buses, trams, trains, and ships. The city's fastest route is the Jeddah-Riyadh Highway, which connects the two major cities in just 40 minutes.
